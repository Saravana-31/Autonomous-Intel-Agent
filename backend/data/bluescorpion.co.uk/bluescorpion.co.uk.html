Blue Scorpion

Blue Scorpion's roots come from over 55 years' combined in-house expertise working with major retailers, Acquirers and payment service providers. Here at Blue Scorpion, we use our extensive industry knowledge to help retailers maintain, or gain, their Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) compliance quickly and cost effectively through managed services.

We have various services on offer, including IP Protect, IP Protect Lite and Cyber Protect (Blue Scorpion's latest innovation providing segregation wirelessly through a single port WiFi router), WiFi services and Payment solutions that can work flexibly around you.

Want to know more? Call to us today on 01732 602 111 to find out how we can help, or email sales@bluescorpion.co.uk

The benefits of choosing Blue Scorpion

  • Cost-effective solutions:
