Levels of Study

1. Pre-Primary Level(Pre K.G to UKG)

2. Primary Level (I to V)

3. Middle Level (VI to VIII)

5. Higher Secondary Level (XI & XII)

Pre Primary Level (Pre K.G to U.K.G)

Singing, dancing, drawing, colouring, pasting, retelling stories, and activities involving identifying colours, objects, things, and shapes are all included. A fun and thorough learning experience is provided by using the Playway technique and cardboard.

Primary Level (I to V)

Subjects: Tamil, English, Maths, Science, Social Science, Hindi, Moral Education, Computer Science, Physical Education, Fine Arts, General Knowledge, Drawing and Spoken English.

Middle Level (V to VIII)

Subjects: Tamil, English, Maths, Science, Social Science, Hindi, General Knowledge, Computer Science, Physical Education, Drawing, Moral Education and Spoken English.

Secondary Level (IX & X)

Subjects: Tamil, English, Maths, Science, Social Science

Higher Secondary Level (XI & XII)

Group 1:Code 2503:Tamil, English, Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Biology

Group 2:Code 2502: Tamil, English, Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science

Group 3:Code 2601: Tamil, English, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computer Science

Group 4:Code 2702: Tamil, English, Accountancy, Economics, Commerce, Computer Application
