Shri Maruthi Matriculation Higher Secondary School

Spanning a time line of 33 years since its inception by MR.JAYARAMAN in 1990, Shri Maruthi matriculation higher secondary school has been making strides in the field of school education in KrishnaGiri district producing responsible and promising citizens who have proved the schools worth and their mettle and have made their mark in multifarious fields.

Shri maruthi matriculation has always been a source of progress and has nurtured, developed and produced students of great calibre and high quality to the society. It has provided a solid foundation to all the aspiring students who have their roots strongly placed here.

More About School

Motto

Our motto is to equip our children to develop academically and socially to succeed in school and beyond.

Acquiring Marks is not only our motto, but also acquiring knowledge and wisdom.

Vision

To provide the best education possible at an affordable cost and to provide great and responsible citizens to our Society

Mission

To inculcate values and to provide avenues for multi skill development and to bring out the talents of Shri maruthians to step into their future.
